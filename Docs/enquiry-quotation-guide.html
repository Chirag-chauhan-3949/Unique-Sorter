Adding Multiple Items

How to add, remove, and manage items in the enquiry.

Each enquiry can have one or more items. An item represents a single sorting machine the customer is interested in.

RuleDetail
Minimum items1 — the form always starts with one item, and you cannot remove the last one
Maximum itemsNo limit — you can add as many as needed
"+ Add Item" buttonAdds a new empty row with model, size, quantity, and price fields
Remove buttonOnly appears when there are 2 or more items. Removes that specific item.
Required fields per itemModel (required), Quantity (required, at least 1). Size and price are optional / auto-filled.
💡
When the state changes, all items' prices recalculate at once. When you change a model or size, only that specific item's price updates.

Immediate vs Future Requirement

Two different modes based on the customer's timeline.

The form asks: "Does the customer have an immediate requirement?" The answer changes what fields appear:

Yes — Immediate Requirement
The customer wants to buy now. You enter:
  • Items — model, size, quantity, price
  • Can add multiple items
  • Prices auto-calculate
  • This enquiry can generate a quotation
No — Future Requirement
The customer is interested but not ready. You enter:
  • Commodity — Rice, Pulses, Multiproduct, etc.
  • Notes for follow-up
  • Next follow-up date
  • Probable month of order
  • % Chances of order

Saving an Enquiry

What happens when you click "Save Enquiry".

1
Validation Check
The system checks all required fields. If anything is missing or invalid (e.g. phone number not 10 digits), the page scrolls automatically to the first error so you can fix it.
2
Confirmation Dialog
If all fields are valid, a review dialog appears showing all entered details organized by section: Customer Info, Location, Requirement (with items or future details), and Remarks.
3
Review & Confirm
You can click "Edit" to go back and change something, or "Confirm & Save" to submit the enquiry.
4
Form Resets
After successful save, the form automatically clears to empty, ready for the next enquiry. A success message confirms the save.
💡
No data is lost: The enquiry is saved to the database before the form resets. You can find it immediately in the Enquiry dashboard.

How Quotations Work

From enquiry to professional PDF in seconds.

A quotation is a formal price offer sent to the customer. The system can generate two types:

1-Page Quotation
A single A4 page with:
  • Company header + reference numbers
  • Customer details
  • Product table (model, qty, price)
  • GST breakdown + total
  • Amount in words
  • Terms & conditions
  • Bank details + signature
Best for: Quick quotes, simple orders
6-Page Detailed Quotation
A comprehensive 6-page document with:
  • 2-page cover letter introducing PINNACLE series
  • Full technical specifications table
  • Commercial offer + GST + terms
  • Detailed terms & bank details
  • Force majeure clause + signature
Best for: Large orders, formal tenders

GST & Price Calculation

How the system calculates GST and the final total.

Formula

CalculationFormulaExample (Base = ₹31,35,593)
Base PriceEntered or auto-filled₹ 31,35,593
GST AmountBase Price x GST Rate₹ 5,64,407 (at 18%)
TotalBase Price + GST Amount₹ 37,00,000

GST Amount and Total are auto-calculated and cannot be typed manually. They update instantly when you change the base price or GST rate.

A pricing panel appears on the right side of the form showing the breakdown in real-time whenever the base price is greater than zero.

Amount in Words

How the total is converted to words for the PDF.

The system automatically converts the total amount into Indian-format words. For example:

AmountIn Words
₹ 37,00,000Rs. Thirty Seven Lakh Only.
₹ 47,20,000Rs. Forty Seven Lakh Twenty Thousand Only.
₹ 1,25,00,000Rs. One Crore Twenty Five Lakh Only.

This appears on the quotation PDF next to the total amount. The conversion uses the Indian numbering system: Crore → Lakh → Thousand → Hundred.

PDF Generation

How the quotation becomes a downloadable PDF.

1
Click "Preview"
The system builds the quotation layout using your form data and displays it in a full-screen preview modal, exactly as it will appear on paper (A4 size).
2
Click "Download PDF"
The system captures the preview as a high-resolution image and converts it into a PDF file. The file downloads to your device automatically.

The PDF includes the company header, stamp image, and all form data laid out professionally on A4 paper. For 6-page quotations, all 6 pages are captured and combined into a single PDF file.

Sharing via WhatsApp & Email

How to send the quotation directly to the customer.

After generating a quotation, you can share it from the preview screen:

WhatsApp
Opens WhatsApp with a pre-filled message containing the quotation details. You attach the downloaded PDF and send it to the customer's number.
Gmail
Opens Gmail with a pre-filled subject line and message body. Attach the PDF to complete the email and send.
