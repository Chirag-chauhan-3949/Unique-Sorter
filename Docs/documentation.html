Unique Sorter CRM

A complete guide to using the CRM platform built for Unique Sorter & Equipment Pvt. Ltd. — from field engineer enquiry submissions to generating professional quotations.

Overview

What this platform does and who it is for.

The Unique Sorter CRM is a web-based platform that helps the team at Unique Sorter And Equipment Pvt. Ltd. manage their entire sales workflow in one place. Field engineers on site collect customer requirements and enter them into the system. The back-office team then reviews enquiries, generates quotations, and shares professional PDFs with clients.

Enquiry Management
Field engineers capture customer requirements on site through a structured form. Track each enquiry with full details, items, pricing, and follow-up dates.
Quotation Generation
Convert enquiries into professional, GST-compliant PDF quotations with one click. Share via WhatsApp or email instantly.
Team Collaboration
All team members can view enquiries, edit details, and generate quotations. Admins get additional settings control.
Secure Access
Phone-based OTP login ensures only verified team members can access the dashboard. No passwords to remember.

Submitting an Enquiry

For field engineers — how to enter a customer's requirement after a site visit.

After visiting a client, log into the CRM and navigate to the enquiry form. You can access it from the "+ New Enquiry" button on the enquiry dashboard, or directly via the /enquiry page. Login is required to submit enquiries.

What the form asks for

Section 1 — Customer Information

Customer Name (required)
Mill / Company Name (required)
Mobile Number (required)
Email Address (optional)
GST Number (optional)
Lead Source (dropdown)

Section 2 — Location Details

City / Location (required)
State (required)
Full Address (required)

Section 3 — Requirement

You will be asked: "Do you have an immediate requirement?"

✅
If YES (Immediate): You can add one or more sorting machine items with Model (Pinnacle / Nandak), Size (5–10 chutes), Quantity, and Price. Prices are auto-calculated based on your state and chosen model.
🕑
If NO (Future): You can specify the commodity type (Rice, Pulses, etc.), expected month of order, chances of order (%), and any notes for follow-up.

Section 4 — Remarks (optional additional notes)

After filling everything, click "Save". You will see a success confirmation. The enquiry is now stored in the system and the back-office team can review it, generate quotations, and follow up.

Registration

How to request access to the CRM.

1
Go to the Registration Page
Click "Register here" from the login page, or navigate directly to /register.
2
Fill in Your Details
Enter your full name, phone number (10 digits, starting with 6–9), choose your role (User or Admin), and set a password. You can toggle password visibility with the eye icon.
3
Wait for Admin Approval
Your registration request is submitted with pending status. You cannot login until an administrator approves your account from the Settings page.
4
Login After Approval
Once approved, you can sign in using either OTP or password login. You will be notified to contact your admin if you try to login before approval.
⚠️
No self-registration: Every new account requires admin approval before it becomes active. This ensures only authorized team members can access the CRM. Admins can also create users directly from Settings (these are auto-approved).

Login (OTP or Password)

Two ways to sign in — choose whichever is more convenient.

The login page has a toggle switch at the top to choose between OTP and Password login.

Method 1: Phone + OTP

1
Enter Your Phone Number
Type your 10-digit phone number. The country code +91 is added automatically.
2
Receive OTP via SMS
Click "Send OTP". A 6-digit code is sent to your phone via SMS.
3
Enter the 6-Digit Code & Sign In
Type the code into the 6 boxes (auto-advances). You can also paste the full code. If the code expires, click "Resend OTP" after the 60-second timer.

Method 2: Phone/Email + Password

1
Switch to Password Tab
Click the "Password" tab at the top of the login card.
2
Enter Phone or Email + Password
Enter your phone number (e.g. 9876543210) or email address in the first field, then enter your password. Use the eye icon to show/hide the password.
3
Click Sign In
You are logged in instantly and redirected to the dashboard. No OTP needed.

Forgot Password?

If you forget your password, click "Forgot Password?" below the password form. The reset flow:

1
Enter Your Phone Number
An OTP is sent to verify your identity.
2
Verify OTP
Enter the 6-digit code from SMS.
3
Set New Password
Enter and confirm your new password (minimum 6 characters). The new password is securely saved.

Menu Item What It Does Access

Managing Enquiries

How to view, edit, filter, and act on customer enquiries.

Viewing Enquiries

Navigate to Enquiry in the sidebar. You'll see a table listing all enquiries with columns:

Customer Name
Requirement (Immediate / Future)
Mill / Company
Mobile Number
Location
Date

While loading, animated skeleton placeholders appear instead of a spinner, so you can see the table structure taking shape.

Searching & Filtering

Use the search bar and filter panel at the top to narrow down enquiries by customer name, location, requirement type, or date range.

Viewing Details

Click any row to open the full enquiry detail page. Here you can see all the information the field engineer recorded, including items, pricing, and notes.

Editing an Enquiry

On the detail page, click the three-dot menu (top-right) and select "Edit". Make your changes and click "Save". All edits are tracked.

Creating a New Enquiry

Click the "+ New Enquiry" button at the top of the enquiry list. This opens the enquiry form where you can enter the customer's details collected during a site visit.

Deleting an Enquiry

From the three-dot menu on the detail page, select "Delete". You'll be asked to confirm before the enquiry is permanently removed.

Quotations & PDF Generation

How to generate, customise, and share professional quotations.

Generating a Quotation from an Enquiry

1
Open an Enquiry
Go to the enquiry list and click any enquiry to open its detail page.
2
Click "Generate Quotation"
At the bottom of the detail page, click the "Generate Quotation" button. The system creates a new quotation and auto-fills all customer info, product details, and pricing from the enquiry.
3
Review & Customise
You're taken to the quotation editor where you can adjust any field — reference numbers, pricing, GST rate, terms & conditions, delivery details, and more.
4
Preview, Save & Share
Click "Preview" to see the full A4 quotation. From there, download as PDF, share via WhatsApp, or send by email.

Quotation Fields

The quotation form has five sections:

Section What It Contains
Reference Ref Number, Ref Date, Quotation Number, Quotation Date
Client Details Contact Person, Company, Address, City, State, Mobile, Email
Product Details Model, Quantity, Description, HSN Code, Base Price, Exclusions
Pricing & GST GST Rate (0–28%), GST Amount (auto), Total (auto), Amount in Words
Terms Payment Terms, Delivery Point, Lead Time, Commodity, Validity, Warranty, Bank Details

Sharing Options

WhatsApp
Share the quotation PDF directly to a WhatsApp contact or group with a pre-filled message.
Gmail / Email
Opens Gmail with pre-filled subject and body. Attach the downloaded PDF to complete the email.

Admin Settings

Platform configuration available to administrators only.

The Settings page is accessible only to users with the Admin role. It appears in the sidebar navigation only for admins.

User Management

Admins can create, edit, and delete users. For each user, the admin can:

Set name, phone, password, and role
Approve pending registrations (green checkmark button)
Configure per-user screen access
Remove users from the system

Approving New Users

When someone registers, their account shows as "Pending" with an amber badge. The admin clicks the green checkmark to approve them. Only approved users can login.

Users created directly by an admin from Settings are auto-approved immediately.

⚠️
If you see an "Access Denied" message when trying to open Settings, your account has the User role. Ask an administrator to upgrade your access if needed.

Per-User Screen Access

Admin controls which dashboard screens each user can see.

1
Admin Opens Settings → Edit User
Navigate to Settings and click the Edit button on any user with the "User" role.
2
Toggle Screen Checkboxes
Below the Role selector, a "Screen Access" section shows checkboxes for each screen: Dashboard, Enquiry, Quotations, Profile, Reports, Products, Orders, Inventory, Clients. Dashboard and Profile are always on.
3
Save — User Sees Only Allowed Screens
The user's sidebar updates to show only their allowed screens. Visiting a blocked URL redirects them to the dashboard.
💡
Default: If no restrictions are set, the user sees all screens their role allows. Admins always see everything regardless of this setting.

CSV Export

Download all enquiries or quotations as a CSV file (admin-only).

Admins see a "CSV" button on both the Enquiry and Quotations pages. Clicking it downloads all records as a CSV file that opens in Excel or Google Sheets.

ExportFile NameIncludes
Enquiries enquiries-YYYY-MM-DD.csv Customer info, location, up to 3 items with model/size/qty/price, future requirement fields, remarks, date
Quotations quotations-YYYY-MM-DD.csv Quote/ref numbers, type, client details, product info, GST breakdown, all terms & conditions, dates

Bulk Select & Delete

Select multiple enquiries or quotations and delete them at once (admin-only).

1
Select Items
A checkbox column appears for admins. Click individual checkboxes or use the header checkbox to select all. Selected rows are highlighted in blue.
2
Floating Action Bar Appears
A dark bar slides up at the bottom of the screen showing how many items are selected, with Delete and Cancel buttons.
3
Confirm & Delete
Click Delete to see a confirmation dialog. Confirm to permanently remove all selected items. This action cannot be undone.
⚠️
When items are selected, clicking a row toggles its selection instead of navigating to the detail page. Click "Cancel" in the action bar to clear the selection and return to normal navigation.

Data & Storage

Where your data lives and how it is protected.

Firebase Firestore
All data (user accounts, enquiries, quotations) is stored in Google's Firebase Firestore — a secure, cloud-hosted database.
Firebase Authentication
Login is handled by Firebase Auth with phone-based OTP. No passwords are stored in the database. Sessions are encrypted with JWT tokens.

Data Collections

CollectionWhat It Stores
userdataUser profiles — name, phone, role, creation date
enquiriesAll enquiries — customer info, items, pricing, notes, dates
quotationsAll quotations — client details, product info, terms, pricing

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions and quick answers.

I'm not receiving the OTP. What should I do?

Wait for the full 60-second timer to expire, then click "Resend OTP". Ensure your phone number is correct and has SMS reception. You can also try the Password login method instead.

I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

On the login page, switch to the Password tab and click "Forgot Password?". Enter your phone number, verify with OTP, then set a new password.

I registered but can't login. Why?

New registrations require admin approval. Your account is in "pending" status until an admin approves it from the Settings page. Contact your administrator.

Can I login with my email address?

Yes. On the Password tab, you can enter either your phone number or email address along with your password. Your email must be stored in your account (set by admin).

Can customers see the dashboard?

No. Every page except the homepage, login, and registration requires authentication. Customers never interact with the platform directly.

Can I edit a quotation after saving it?

Yes. Open the quotation from the quotations list and modify any field. You can re-download or re-share the updated PDF.

How are prices calculated?

Prices are auto-filled based on a lookup table that uses the customer's state, machine model, and size (chutes). These can be manually overridden in the quotation editor if needed.

Who can see my data?

All logged-in team members can see all enquiries and quotations, unless the admin has restricted their screen access. It's a shared team workspace.

How do I delete multiple enquiries at once?

Admins can use bulk select. Check the checkboxes next to the enquiries you want to remove, then click the red "Delete" button in the floating action bar at the bottom of the screen.

Is the platform mobile-friendly?

Yes. Every page is fully responsive. On phones, the sidebar becomes a slide-out menu, tables adapt to show key info in a compact layout, and all forms are touch-friendly.

What browsers are supported?

Any modern browser — Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge. We recommend keeping your browser updated for the best experience.
